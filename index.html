Liqian Chen

I am an Associate Professor in the College of Computer Science at the National University of Defense Technology (NUDT), Changsha, China.

E-mail: lqchen AT nudt.edu.cn

Research Interests:

    My research interests include program analysis/verification, abstract interpretation, automated program repair.
