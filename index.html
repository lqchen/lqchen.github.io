Liqian Chen

I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Computer Science at the National University of Defense Technology (NUDT), Changsha, China.

E-mail: liqianchen AT gmail.com

Research Interests:

    My research interests include program analysis/verification, abstract interpretation. In particular, I am interested in numerical abstract domains and numerical static analysis.
