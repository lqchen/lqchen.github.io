Liqian Chen

I am an Associate Professor in the College of Computer at the National University of Defense Technology (NUDT), Changsha, China.

E-mail: liqianchen AT gmail.com

Research Interests:

    My research interests include program analysis/verification, abstract interpretation, automated program repair.

Software:

  • AutoRNP: a dynamic analysis tool for automatically detecting and repairing high floating-point errors in numerical programs.
